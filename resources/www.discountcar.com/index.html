Car and Truck Rentals

Established in 1980, Discount Car and Truck Rentals is proud to be Canadian owned and operated with more than 300 locations across the country to serve you. We offer easy car and truck rental solutions for all your needs, whether it's to run errands, insurance replacement vehicles, a road trip with the family or truck rentals to move large furniture or move your entire house! Discount is proud to always offer the best rates and special offers. Customers can also earn and redeem Petro-Points every time they rent a car or rent a truck!

Our Car and Truck Rental Fleet Options

Our car rental services include a variety of vehicle options for any purpose or occasion such as touring cities, road trips, weddings, anniversaries, corporate functions and more. We offer options for economy or compact class cars, large SUVs or pick-up trucks, as well as premium and luxury car rentals that can seat as little as three or as many as seven passengers.

Our moving trucks and vans are late model year, well maintained and offer an easy do-it-yourself way to move your belongings across town or across the country. Our trucks are also perfect for any business needs. Our fleet offers ½ ton, ¾ ton to 1 ton pick-up trucks, 16 to 26 foot cube trucks, cargo vans with various roof heights, as well as refrigerated trucks to keep your cargo at the right temperature. We also specialize in site-ready trucks for many industries across Canada. We offer national and local account management and can help with all your short and long term project needs with a customizable commercial fleet.

Whether you are moving short or long distances, attending an event, needing transportation at college, or travelling to international locations, Discount can help you find the perfect car or truck rental solution.
