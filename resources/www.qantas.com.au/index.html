Classic Rewards

Classic Rewards provide members with a great way to fly for a fixed points level, and are available on eligible flights on Qantas, Jetstar, Emirates, oneworld® alliance airlines and selected Qantas Frequent Flyer airline partners and affiliates. Classic Rewards are not available on some routes and some restrictions may apply. Selecting Classic Rewards will allow you to widen your search and check Classic Reward availability on Qantas and our airline partners, including the ability to book multi-city or multi cabin itineraries, and destinations only available to be booked online as Classic Rewards.

Search only for Classic Flight Reward availability on Qantas, Jetstar, and a wider range of Emirates, oneworld® alliance airlines and selected Qantas Frequent Flyer airline partners and affiliates. Restrictions may apply.
