        Cricket Cell Phones and Plans: No Contract for Annual Service

        With Cricket's prepaid cell phones and plans, there's no contract for annual phone service because payments are month to month. Plus, taxes and fees are included in your bill, so you can easily budget your phone expenses.

        What are the benefits of prepaid cell phone plans?
        The biggest benefit is flexibility. With Cricket's cell phone plans, you can update your service with ease and choose the device you want. So, bring your favorite GSM-compatible cell phone to Cricket - or find the latest prepaid smartphones for sale, including models from some of the best brands like Samsung, iPhone, and Nokia.

        How does Cricket's prepaid service compare with traditional post-paid service?
        We offer many of the features post-paid carriers offer, only with affordable prices and no contract annually. With Cricket you get: (1) more 4G LTE coverage nationwide than Boost, Sprint, or T-Mobile, (2) unlimited talk and text, and (3) a reliable network you can count on.
