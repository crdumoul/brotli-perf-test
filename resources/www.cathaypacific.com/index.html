Economy

From 1 March 2016, passengers aged between 12 and 15 travelling in Economy class will be exempted from the UK Air Passenger Duty (APD) Tax. We are currently updating our system to reflect the change. If you want to make a booking or have an existing one for a passenger aged 12-15 departing the UK on or after 1 March 2016, please contact the local reservations office or Global Contact Centres for reservations, or APD Tax refunds.

Please note that you currently cannot make a group booking (of more than six people) online. To proceed, please contact your local reservations office on 1 (800) 268-68681 (800) 268-6868.
